Hi There!

My name is Ta Ying Cheng (Tim) and I am a 4th year computer science student studying in the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I conducted my final year project on implementing a lightweight real-time 3D reconstruction, supervised by the HKUST VisGraph Lab and Professor Long Quan. Before that I was interning the HKUST HCI Initiative, supervised by Professor Ma Xiaojuan.

My research interest is in 3D computer vision, deep learning in computer vision and their applications (AR/VR).
I sometimes write article about projects and research I enjoy too! You can read them here.
